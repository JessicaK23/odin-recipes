Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti carbonara is made up of pasta and is often made with cream and other ingredients. It is a roman classic and differs from the modern spaghetti carbonara recipe.


Ingredients

  • 200 g dried spaghetti
  • 1 clove og garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 large free-range egg yolks
  • 1 x 150 g piece of higher-welfare pancetta
  • 40 g Parmesan cheese , plus extra to serve
