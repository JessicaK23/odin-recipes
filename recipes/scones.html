Scones

Description

Scones are baked goods,that are usually made of either oatmeal or wheat, along with other ingredients and baking powder as a leavening agent. They are rolled in to a round shape and cut into quarters before baking. Scones are baked on sheet pans and can often taste slightly sweet.


Ingredients

  • 25g/1 oz Butter or Margarine
  • 150ml/1/4pt Milk (approx)
  • 25g/1 oz Shamrock Golden Caster Sugar (optional)
  • 225g/8oz Odlums Self Raising Flour
  • Pinch of Salt
